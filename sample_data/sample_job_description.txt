Senior ML Engineer – AI Platform Team
TechCorp AI | San Francisco, CA | Full-time

ABOUT THE ROLE
We are looking for a Senior ML Engineer to join our AI Platform team. You will design, build, and scale machine learning systems that power our core products used by millions of users worldwide.

RESPONSIBILITIES
- Design and implement end-to-end machine learning pipelines from data ingestion to model deployment
- Build and maintain MLOps infrastructure for model training, evaluation, and serving
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams (product, data science, engineering) in an agile environment
- Lead technical design discussions and mentor junior engineers
- Drive A/B testing and experimentation frameworks
- Optimize model performance and system scalability

REQUIRED SKILLS & EXPERIENCE
- 5+ years software engineering experience with 2+ years in ML engineering
- Strong programming skills in Python; familiarity with TypeScript or JavaScript is a plus
- Experience with ML frameworks: TensorFlow, PyTorch, scikit-learn, or XGBoost
- Proficiency in cloud platforms: AWS, GCP, or Azure
- Experience with containerization and orchestration: Docker, Kubernetes
- Strong knowledge of SQL and experience with data warehouses (BigQuery, Redshift, or Snowflake)
- Experience with data pipeline tools: Apache Airflow, Spark, or dbt
- Familiarity with NLP and large language models (LLMs) is highly preferred
- Knowledge of MLOps practices and tools
- Strong communication and leadership skills
- Experience with agile and scrum methodologies

NICE TO HAVE
